Project: Web-Design

Körnig: Creating an Organic Juice and Smoothie Shop Website

The goal of this project was to create a responsive website for Körnig, a fictitious organic business that sells juice and smoothies in the center of Würzburg. The objective was to develop a contemporary and captivating online presence that emphasizes the store's dedication to organic products and its status as a family-owned enterprise.
There were three stages to the project:
✔ Concept Phase: The main idea is developed, personas are created, a sitemap is created, and interactive wireframes are created to specify the structure of the website.
✔ Design Phase: Investigation of several design variants, encompassing color schemes, typography, and logo development. Clear user instructions and contemporary aesthetics played a major role in the final design's selection.
✔ Finalization Phase: Making adjustments to the button sizes, spacing, and interactive components for both desktop and mobile versions. Figma Mirror testing is used to ensure usability.
The project gave me important knowledge about responsive web design, user-centered development, and UI/UX design. It was a fantastic opportunity to learn how to balance style and utility.
